Introduction of the Marketplace
Crabada Marketplace
Buy and Sell Crabs
The Marketplace provides a user-friendly interface for players to interact with Crabada assets.
Players can browse through listings of Crabada to buy, as well as list their own Crabada for sale. Players sell Crabada by creating a listing, with a specified ask price. Buyers are able to purchase the Crabada by spending TUS from his wallet. Upon confirmation of the transaction on the blockchain, the settlement is settled instantaneously. The Seller receives TUS in his wallet, and the Crabada is transferred to the Buyer. The native currency used for transactions will be TUS tokens.
Sellers are charged a 3.85% fee on completion of a transaction.
